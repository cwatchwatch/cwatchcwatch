How CoffeeGuard Works

CoffeeGuard is a discreet device that integrates with your existing coffee machines to monitor and record every coffee served. Our system provides:

  • Real-time tracking of all coffee servings
  • Instant alerts to your dashboard when discrepancies occur
  • Detailed reports showing daily, weekly, and monthly coffee counts
  • Secure cloud storage of all your data
  • Access from any device - phone, tablet, or computer

With CoffeeGuard, you'll never have to worry about stolen coffee or accounting mistakes again. Our system helps Moroccan coffee shop owners recover thousands of dirhams in lost revenue each year.
